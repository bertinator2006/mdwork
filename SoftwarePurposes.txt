# VaultRE 
## Software Purpose & Benefits
- source-of-truth database that holds
	- prospect clients
	- current clients
	- prospect properties
	- listed properties
	- notes on prospects
	- sharing contacts
	- storing office tradies and other such contacts
- can be used for other purposes but the above is mandated
- automations that trigger when certain actions are taken in the CRM
- Mandated templates that need to be used at certain steps of the property journey
- Reduces errors in communication
- Speeds up communication between team members for listing and communicating with clients

## Users
- lead agents (mandated)
- assistant agents (optional)
- all admin and principals (mandated)

## Multi Dynamic Value
- Integrations pre-setup
- Email signatures already done
- Complete profiles for agents
- Branding to make the UI/UX feel more at home
- Pre-made: Categories; Email and SMS Templates; Action Lists; Designly templates; 
- Specialised training and knowledgable personell available just a phone call away (very important)
