# Prospecting Activities
Sales Agents are required to actively prospect using the following methods:
- Cold Calling
- Door Knocking
- Letterbox drops
- Social media posts
- Just Listed / Just Sold notifications
- Free market appraisals
- Expired and withdrawn listing follow-ups
- Building relationships with local business owners
- Garage sales, Gumtree, similar platforms
- ID4me and RP Data
- Company-provided leads

# Appraisal Appointment & CRM Entry
- Book an appraisal appointment with the potential vendor
- Enter vendor and property details using "Appraisal Wizard" within VaultRE
- Record any important notes, comments, WITH followup reminders within VaultRE

# Appraisal Preparation
When going for an appraisal appointment, ensure you bring the following:
- Comparable price analysis
- Appraisal letter
- 4-6 comparable properties
- Property research and current market updates
- Updated pre-listing kit
## Important:
- Sending pre-listing kit and appointment reminder one day in advance
- Do not send an email appraisal before physically inspecting the property

# Appraisal Appointment Protocol
Bring the following:
- Multi Dynamic Folder
- Appraisal letter
- Agency agreement
- DL and A4 Brochures

Professional Conduct:
- Arrive at least 5 minutes early
- Be well-presented and prepared

Collect the following details:
- Land Size
- Building size

# Pre-Listing & Agency Agreement
- Send an updated proposal including:
	- selling process
	- Marketing pland and marketing fees
- Provide the vendor fact sheet prior to signing any exclusive agency agreement
- Ensure the exclusive agency agreement is fully completed and signed by all parties (Vendors listed on the contract and agent)
- Create a Physical file:
	- Original copy retained in the office
	- Copy provided to the vendor
- If using docusign, prepare and send al required documents
Upload the following onto VaultRE:
- Property details
- Agency agreement
- Vendor information
- Property notes (Features and inclusion)

# Compliance & Audit
- Create a property compliance folder and audit checklist
- Follow the Multi Dynamic Audit Checklist and state property law provision guidelines
- Request vendor instructions to prepare the contract of sale
