# Prospecting Activities
Sales Agents are required to actively prospect using the following methods:
- Cold Calling
- Door Knocking
- Letterbox drops
- Social media posts
- Just Listed / Just Sold notifications
- Free market appraisals
- Expired and withdrawn listing follow-ups
- Building relationships with local business owners
- Garage sales, Gumtree, similar platforms
- ID4me and RP Data
- Company-provided leads

# Appraisal Appointment & CRM Entry
- Book an appraisal appointment with the potential vendor
- Enter vendor and property details using "Appraisal Wizard" within VaultRE
- Record any important notes, comments, WITH followup reminders within VaultRE

# Appraisal Preparation
When going for an appraisal appointment, ensure you bring the following:
- Comparable price analysis
- Appraisal letter
- 4-6 comparable properties
- Property research and current market updates
- Updated pre-listing kit
## Important:
- Sending pre-listing kit and appointment reminder one day in advance
- Do not send an email appraisal before physically inspecting the property

# Appraisal Appointment Protocol
Bring the following:
- Multi Dynamic Folder
- Appraisal letter
- Agency agreement
- DL and A4 Brochures

Professional Conduct:
- Arrive at least 5 minutes early
- Be well-presented and prepared

Collect the following details:
- Land Size
- Building size

# Pre-Listing & Agency Agreement
- Send an updated proposal including:
	- selling process
	- Marketing pland and marketing fees
- Provide the vendor fact sheet prior to signing any exclusive agency agreement
- Ensure the exclusive agency agreement is fully completed and signed by all parties (Vendors listed on the contract and agent)
- Create a Physical file:
	- Original copy retained in the office
	- Copy provided to the vendor
- If using docusign, prepare and send al required documents
Upload the following onto VaultRE:
- Property details
- Agency agreement
- Vendor information
- Property notes (Features and inclusion)

# Compliance & Audit
- Create a property compliance folder and audit checklist
- Follow the Multi Dynamic Audit Checklist and state property law provision guidelines
- Request vendor instructions to prepare the contract of sale

# Post-Cooling-Off Period
Once the cooling of period expires:
- Schedule a sales strategy meeting with the vendor (Set to sell meeting)
- Discuss marketing, communication, and value creation strategies
- Create a group chat with: agents and vendors

# Pre-Marketing Activities
- Deliver 40 "Coming Soon - Dear Neighbour" letters
- Door knock and cold call using the 10-10-20 rule the following day
- Issue marketing invoice to the vendor (if applicable)
- Coordinate photography with the vendor and MD Photographer
- Ensure photos are uploaded to the CRM
- Prepare property content for online marketing

# Marketing & Listing
- Upload Property Photos to VaultRE
- Order signboards and flyers through Printforce only
- Use approved Multi Dynamic signage and flyers only
- Double-check all property and agent details before approval
- Prepare a property profile including:
	- Land size
	- Year built
	- Council, strata, and water details
Once the contract of sale is received:
- Upload it to the CRM
- Print a copy with MD-branded cover page
- Commence marketing campaign

# Buyer Management & Inspections
- Arrange inspection times with the vendor
- Notify buyer matches via CRM email
- Call VIP buyers and invite private inspections
- Email the vendor for final approval before going live

## Open for inspection Kit
- Contract for Sale (minimum 5 pages)
- Property profile
- A4 and DL fluers
- Client registration list
- Property QR code printed copy
- Updated pre-listing kit
- Keys
- Letter of offer copes
- Authority to exchange copy
Note:
- Listing agents must organise spike sign by Thursday evening
- Collect spike signs after Saturday inspections
- Admin/assistant agent must send inspection invitations via e-newsletters

# Vendor Communication
- Update vendor immediately after inspections (Phone call)
- Send weekly marketing and activities report every monday
- Call the vendor at least twice per week
- If possible, have a vendor physical meeting every 2nd week

# Offers & Acceptance
- All offers must be in writing (email or offer form)
- Present offers to the vendor promptly
If any offers are accepted:
- Prepare sales advice
- Collect buyer photo ID
- Reuqest 0.25% holding deposit into MD Sales Trust Account
- File all documents correctly

# Exchange & Settlement
- Complete and sign authorito to exchange
- Contract black box (if required)
- Issue deposit receipt to buyer and solicitor
- Exchange contracts and send to both solicitors
- Update CRM and Portals as Under Contract
- Assist valuers and inspectors
Upon exchange:
- Confirm 10% or 5% as agreed
- Update CRM and portals to SOLD
- Record settlement date in CRM and calendar

# Settlement & Post-Sale
- Conduct final inspection using MD Final Inspection Checklist
- Update all communications in CRM
Upon settlement confirmation:
- Update CRM
- Release deposit as per solicitor instruction onece you receive the order an agent from the solicitors
- Send congratulations and welcome letters (with feedback link)
- Set a physical meeting with the venfor (seller)
- Set a physical meeting with the buyer
- Update vendor and buyer status in CRM
- Set VIP follow-ups and 6-month appraisal reminders
- Activate anniversary emails
- Prepare and deliver handover kits as per MD Procedure
